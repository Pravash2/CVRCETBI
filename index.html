About us

We are a technology business incubator based in ODISHA supporting and nurturing startup companies by providing services such as incubation, modern office spaces, mentoring , networking opportunities, and rapid prototyping.

We are CVRCE- Technology Business Incubator founded in 2019, a non-profit organization with an integral role in catalyzing the entrepreneurial movement in India. By supporting entrepreneurs and start-up ideas, we will highlight India's reputation as a destination for Entrepreneurship and Innovation.

We will be India`s most sought after Technology Business Incubator committed to growing a collaborative entrepreneurial network by providing a one-stop support system for start-ups and transforming Orissa as a hub for innovation.

Vision

To Promote Ideas, Creativity, And Innovation In Agriculture And Food Technology.

Mission

To Develop And Encourage A Culture Of New Innovations In A Country As A Whole

Our Purpose

To create jobs, wealth and business setup in the area of agriculture (food processing industry) based solutions to fulfil the smart city requirement aligning with national priorities.

To design aggressive marketing strategies for speedy commercialisation of technologies developed by CVRCE TBI.

To channelize our industrial linkages towards development of a vibrant start-up ecosystem.

To utilise the existing legal and IPR cell of the institute for cost effective legal and IPR related services.

To utilise the expertise of our intellectual capital and the facilities of COEs for providing best mentoring system and low cost prototype product development.

To promote new technologies/knowledge/innovation based start-up in the area of food processing to reach out to the unique natural food products of the region to the rest of the world.

Overview

THRUST AREAS

CVRCE- Technology Business Incubator (CVRCE – TBI) , an Odisha Region focused Technology Business Incubator for incubation and enterprise creation in the area of Agriculture and Agro-based Industries with special focus on innovative Food Technologies, Food Processing, and other focus areas:

  1. Precision/Smart Agriculture - Farm Fresh Retail
  2. Innovative Food Technology - Farm Mechanization
  3. Supply Chain Technology - Pest Management
  4. Soil, Water & Weather technology - Soil Management
  5. ICT and IoT in Agriculture - Crop Monitoring
  6. Post-harvest Technology
